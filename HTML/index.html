Help Desk Services

ERROR 404, is a dedicated website catering to the Students of Western Michigan University, with the help of the OIT we have had a pleasure to make a website that includes issues that are faced by the Western community more oftenly.

The help desk provides technical support for these areas:


  • Anti-virus/virus support
  • Classroom technology support
  • Computer, laptop and mobile device support
  • Chat Support
  • Email support
  • Remote Support
  • Software support
  • Walk-in support

There are three full-time supervisors, three full-time phone support staff and numerous student employees working two shifts to provide support to the WMU campus community. In addition, the mobile help desk visits residence halls to assist students with technology issues.
