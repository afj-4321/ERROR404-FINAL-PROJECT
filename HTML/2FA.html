Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Guide

2FA is going to be fully implemented on the 22nd of January 2020. WMU has joined the many universities that use two-factor authentication (2FA) to protect students, faculty, and staff from data and privacy violations. Since 2016, over 1500 WMU accounts have been compromised by criminals who have stolen Bronco NetIDs, passwords, financial information and money. 2FA adds an extra layer of security by sending a notification to your mobile device when you log in so that you may verify that it is you logging in and not a criminal.

When you reach the enrolling page, you are prompted to enter your cellphone number, enter that and choose the type of your device.

Go on to your cellphone and download DUO MOBILE from your phone's app store
After you have downloaded it click the button "I have Duo Mobile Intsalled"

Open up the DUO app and select "Get Started", after you have done that, a camera scanner pops up in the app from where you could scan the barcode given to you in your browser

Now that you have all the procedures for enrolling into 2FA, hop on over to the enrollment page and secure your account!



You might be thinking that this is a hassle, but do not fear since DUO has an option for not asking you for verification every time you sign up, click on "Remember me for 30 days" on your favourite device and never get bothered!
