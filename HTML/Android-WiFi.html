Android WiFi Guide

Adding your Android device to the network is as simple as connecting to WMU Secure on your device and to make sure you don't validate the security certificate, after doing so you must go through the device registration using the link below.
If you need more detailed instructions look through the guide below.

On your Android device open settings, and look for WiFi/connections

Scroll down to WiFi and select it.


(Make sure it on as well)

Scroll down until you see WMU Secure and select it from the list.

select security certificate in the wifi mune and make sure don't validate is select.

After you have selected dont validate close the menu

Enter your Bronco NetID into the identity field and your password in the password field.

Scrool donw and selectconnect.

Now that your on the WiFi you need to finish by registering your device, you can hit the button or use the QR code.



To use the QR code simply open your camera on your phone and point it at the code.
